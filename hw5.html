Cats are the best animals. There are outdoor and indoor cats, and while cat owners should not try to domesticate outdoor cats, they can support them. Cats can be a great source of friendship. They are fluffy and cute. They also can teach responsibility. Cats are there for us in our time of need.

cats love us

Cats love being around humans. Although they may be wrongly domesticated, they are companions and give people support. Cats also have different personalities, but they all appreciate owners who treat them well. Cats are also super cute and soft.
